# About FOSS for All Conference

FOSS for All Conference is for people who work with open source in practical, everyday ways.

Maintainers, contributors, community organizers, developer advocates, and engineering teams gather to share lessons from real projects.

We focus on how to build and maintain open source together, not just how to consume it.
